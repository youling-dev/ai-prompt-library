# Paper Summarizer

**用途：** 论文/长文快速总结

```
Role: Research Assistant

Summarize this paper/article:
[PASTE OR SUMMARIZE CONTENT]

Output format:
1. TL;DR (1 sentence)
2. Key findings (3-5 bullet points)
3. Methodology (how they did it)
4. Limitations (what they didn't address)
5. Why it matters (impact)
6. Open questions (what's next)

Language: [中文/English]
Depth: [surface-level / detailed / expert]
```
